📖 ImageFlipbook v1.2.0

Mobile Enhancements Test

🔥 New in v1.2.0:

  • Mobile Fullscreen Fallback - Custom overlay when native fullscreen isn't available
  • Touch Navigation Buttons - Left/right circular buttons in fullscreen
  • Swipe Gesture Support - Swipe left/right to change pages
  • Tap to Advance - Tap center area to go to next page
  • iOS Safari Compatible - Enhanced mobile browser support
  • 44px Touch Targets - Mobile accessibility compliant buttons

Test Instructions:

  1. 👆 Tap any page or the "⛶ Full" button to enter fullscreen
  2. 📱 On mobile: You'll see a custom fullscreen overlay
  3. 🎮 Navigate using:
    • Touch the ← → buttons on screen edges
    • Swipe left/right anywhere on the page
    • Tap the center of the page to advance
    • Use ✕ button to exit fullscreen
